Welcome to Leisure Valley!

Take a quick look around or browse at your leisure we are here to accommodate our camping patrons. A mixture of relaxation, entertainment, nature and spontaneity are only a few of the benefits of camping at Leisure Valley. A 71 acre, 106 site campground facility we offer full hook-up to rustic tent sites. Whether the sun shines brightly or the rain makes an appearance, young or old there is something for everyone

  • Modern Club House
  • Basketball Court

  • Boating ($)
  • Wi-FI
  • Fishing
  • Volleyball
  • Bird Watching
  • Nature Trails
  • Relaxation

We give discounts to Passport America, AAA, etc.

If you are looking for a great place to became a seasonal. Call us to find out about our seasonal sites available. Our season starts on April 15th and will go to October 15th. Hope to see you here. Email or call to make reservations.
